INTERVIEW PREPARATION NOTES
Course: Agentic AI / Introduction to AI Engineering

--- TECHNICAL CONCEPTS ---

Machine Learning is a subset of Artificial Intelligence that enables systems
to learn patterns from data without being explicitly programmed.

Supervised Learning uses labelled data to train models. Common algorithms
include Linear Regression, Decision Trees, and Support Vector Machines.

Python is the most widely used language for AI and data science due to its
simplicity and the rich ecosystem of libraries like pandas, NumPy, and scikit-learn.

pandas is a Python library for data manipulation and analysis. It provides
DataFrames which allow easy data cleaning, filtering, and transformation.

NumPy provides support for large multi-dimensional arrays and mathematical
operations — it is the foundation of most scientific Python libraries.

GitHub is a platform for version control using Git. Employers expect candidates
to have a clean GitHub profile with documented projects.

--- INTERVIEW TIPS ---

Always explain your projects in 2 minutes using the STAR method:
Situation, Task, Action, Result.

Prepare at least 3 strong project examples before any technical interview.

Research the company before the interview — understand their product,
their tech stack, and recent news about them.

Ask the interviewer at least 2 questions at the end — it shows genuine interest.

--- BEHAVIOURAL TIPS ---

Be honest about what you do not know. Saying "I am not sure but I would
approach it like this" is better than guessing incorrectly.

Show enthusiasm for learning — interviewers value growth mindset over
perfect knowledge.

Communication and teamwork are rated as highly as technical skills at
most companies, especially for internship roles.
